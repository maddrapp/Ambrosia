Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about Ambrosia.

Is Ambrosia personal training?

Ambrosia is broader than personal training. It is a whole-person wellness system that integrates movement, recovery, education, and lifestyle support.

Do I need a gym?

No. Plans can be built for private settings, public gyms, outdoor environments, or hybrid routines.

Do you work with injuries?

Ambrosia supports injury prevention, corrective exercise, and movement modification within a wellness scope of practice.

Is this only for athletes?

No. The system is designed for active duty military, first responders, healthcare professionals, busy adults, and anyone pursuing sustainable wellness.
